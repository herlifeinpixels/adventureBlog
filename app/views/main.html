#ExperienceEverything

Visual blog of Hannah, aka. herlifeinpixels

Hi, I'm Hannah from in Toronto, Canada. I have a fearless appetite for adrenaline and adventure. #ExperienceEverything is where I share my stories of meeting familar strangers, experiencing unfamilar things.

#ExperienceEverything is an experiment on the idea that if we allow ourselves to get a little lost, interesting people and experiences will find us and enlighten our lives. The hard part is getting lost and becoming comfortable with that state of mind. I'm exploring this idea of slowing down and celebrating these small, beautiful moments of being present, and getting lost in doing things we really love.
